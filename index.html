Founded in 1856 as the Genovia Agricultural College, the University of Genovia is the state’s flagship and land-grant university. The university is home to more than 39,000 students, 10,000 faculty and staff, and 300 academic programs across 12 schools and colleges. Our students are innovators, entrepreneurs, creators and doers. Togetehr, our faculty and students make Genovia a global leader in research, entrepreneurship and innovation.

About Genovia

Genovia is chock full o’ cities to see. Danberry is the second largest city, offering many parks to stroll in. King Herbert’s Island is off the coast of Genovia and arguably the biggest tourist attraction with its fancy hotels and sandy beaches.
